#### :sunny:  &nbsp; Sun
Grow more vigorously in bright or medium light but tolerate low light well

</br></br>

#### :ocean:  &nbsp; Water
They don’t like to be too wet - water when the top 1-2 inches is dry and empty the saucer after a few minutes to prevent rot

</br></br>

well-draining

</br></br>

#### :yum:  &nbsp; Fertilizer
